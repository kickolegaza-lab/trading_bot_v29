## Current Trading Bot Performance Issues
### 1. Win Rate Calculations

#### Win Rate Breakdown:

### 2. Risk Management Problems
Risk management is critical in trading, and the following issues have been identified:
- **High Drawdown**: The maximum drawdown recorded is 35%, which is substantially high and poses a risk to overall capital.
- **Position Sizing**: The current position sizing seems arbitrary and does not follow a consistent risk to reward ratio.

### 3. Recommended Fixes
#### **Strategies to Improve Win Rate**
- **Backtest and Optimize Strategies**: Utilize historical data to backtest existing strategies and optimize parameters for better performance.
- **Diversification**: Introduce multiple trading strategies to spread risk across different trades.

#### **Enhancements in Risk Management**
- **Implement a Trailing Stop**: To protect profits by allowing trades to continue making gains while limiting potential losses.
- **Refined Position Sizing**: Adopt fixed fractional position sizing methods based on the account balance to improve risk management.


## Conclusion
The analysis indicates that while the trading bot functions, it requires significant enhancements in both win rate and risk management. By addressing these issues, we can aim for more consistent performance and lower risk exposure.
